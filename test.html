About Résumé

Digital Designer

Hello,
I'm Sara.

I'm an artist and digital designer with a background in art education. My experience as an art teacher, work with an early-stage ed-tech startup, and graduate-level interaction design project work allow me to design solutions that improve engagement and accessibility for diverse users.

Available 2026

I design digital products and websites for startups, nonprofits, and small businesses with meaningful missions.

At work

I ask a lot of questions to understand the problems my clients want to solve and the goals they want to achieve. I create wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes that address those goals, then bring them to life with thoughtful visual interfaces and accessibility built in from the start.

My teaching background means I communicate design decisions clearly — to stakeholders, developers, and users alike. I'm equally comfortable leading research, facilitating workshops, or executing pixel-perfect UI.

In life

When I'm not designing, you can find me making art — painting with watercolors, sketching in notebooks, or exploring new mediums just for the joy of it. I'm a passionate traveler who loves discovering new places and cultures, a dedicated yoga practitioner, and a devoted cat parent. I spend a lot of time with my husband and I'm always on the hunt for the best cup of coffee in town.
